- Developed the PIMA-SIM processing-in-memory evaluation tool on top of NVSIM to extract delay, energy, and area data for non-volatile memory and SRAM PIM technologies.
- Improved PIM-level configuration flexibility by supporting customized row activation schemes and sub-array, mat, and bank-level components.
- Evaluated high-density 28 nm STT-assisted SOT-MRAM, showing 95.4% lower energy, 1.6x speedup, and 65% lower area overhead compared with SRAM.
