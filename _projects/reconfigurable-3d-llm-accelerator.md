category: research

- Developed a 2T oxide-semiconductor non-volatile gain-cell memory enabling in-memory reconfiguration between static and dynamic operations for LLM inference and continual learning.
- Implemented a 3D sandwich integration scheme combining oxide-semiconductor logic and memory layers on CMOS, with reconfigurable FP/INT compute modes and 3D inter-tier dataflow optimization.
- Achieved 32.9 TOPS/W energy efficiency and 102.4 TOPS/mm2 compute density, with 2.3x/3.2x gains over planar CMOS and DRAM baselines.
