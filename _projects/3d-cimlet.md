- Designed 3D-CIMlet, a heterogeneous chiplet framework for in-memory acceleration of edge LLM inference and continual learning, with thermal-aware modeling and multi-die design exploration for 2.5D/3D systems.
- Developed memory-reliability-aware chiplet mapping strategies for heterogeneous integration of RRAM, eDRAM, and hybrid dies across mixed technology nodes.
- Achieved 9.3x/12x energy efficiency improvement and 90.2%/92.5% EDP reduction in 2.5D/3D designs for scalable edge LLM deployment.
